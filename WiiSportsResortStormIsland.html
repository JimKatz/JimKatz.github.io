GOLF


Wii Sports Club:

This is probably the most (and only) highly anticipated feature! I worked on this with fellow modder boz.

This mode allows you to play on the golf courses from Wii Sports Club!
 

ISSUES:

  • To put it simply, the game only allocates a certain amount of memory for golf, a few of the courses come extremely close to exceeding that amount. So we were not able to port over every single tree that exists in the WSC version of the courses
     
  •  "Heavy Rough" or any collision type similar to it does not exist in Wii Sports Resort, so it was changed to a regular rough
     
  • The game is only a few bytes away from exceeding the allocated memory region in Hole 5, so the BG texures had to be removed.
     
  • It's Impossible to fix the lag on Hole 6 without breaking the entire course model.
     
  • Due to how the intro cameras work, we were not able to recreate the original course intros from Wii Sports Club.

     

SWORDPLAY SHOWDOWN


Swordplay Showdown Randomizer:

I spent 2 weeks making this, 2 weeks of time out of my own life, time that I could've spent working on my "demo video" or on RiiMajor,
only for everyone to forget that I even made it literally 10 minutes after my showcase stream of it..

Seriously, what the fuck? How do you even do that? 

It's always the stuff I actually put a ton of work into that almost never gets seen, sometimes never seen at all.

You probably don't even know that Wii Sports: Storm City and RiiPlay exist either. 

In order for this feature to not be yet another long period of time I've spent making something that was completely forgotten about, (like in F3 2021) I've decided to include it in this modpack.
 

This feature randomizes the levels in Swordplay Showdown. Each time you pause the game and hit "restart" the game will randomize the CPU Difficulty Level and Armor Type.

 

There are 3 different settings for this feature. Each one changes a certain aspect of the game.
