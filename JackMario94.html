JackMario94

If you plan to mod any wii games, look out for a user named "JackMario94". He is known to steal textures from released modpacks, change a few textures, and reupload it in his own modpack that he claims he made. He once stole a Cave Story MenuSingle that I made for Mario Kart Wii and put it in one of his modpacks, he even disabled commenting on the video because he knew he would be called out. Be extra careful when modding any of the following games, as he follows them all to look for new content to steal: Wii Sports, Wii Sports Resort, Wii Play, Wii Party, Wii Music, and Wii Fit Plus

 

 

Other stuff in relation:

This is a warning to anybody who plans to make mods for wii games.

There is a user who frequents the pages of Wii Series games on this website by the name "JackMario94". He likes to steal textures and mods from modpacks of various Wii games that are posted on this sites. He retextures the mods and edits text and says that it is his own creation. Sometimes he even straight up steals the mod without any modifications. He has stolen many of the mods from my modpacks and even some of my Mario Kart Wii Mods and put it in his own modpack he says he "made".

Be sure to stay on the lookout for any Wii Game mods this user creates, it might just be an edited version of something you made.
